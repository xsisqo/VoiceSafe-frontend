Privacy Policy

VoiceSafe is designed with a privacy-first architecture. We minimize data storage and never sell personal data.

Data We Process

  • Audio files uploaded for analysis
  • Optional case metadata (title, notes, tags)
  • Technical logs for security and reliability

Audio Storage

Audio samples are processed for analysis purposes only. VoiceSafe does not permanently store audio unless explicitly required for saved case functionality.

Security

All communication uses encrypted HTTPS connections. Access controls and monitoring protect backend services.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support.
