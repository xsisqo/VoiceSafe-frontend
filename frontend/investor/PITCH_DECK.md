# VoiceSafe — Investor Pitch (10 slides)

## Slide 1 — One-liner
VoiceSafe detects voice fraud & AI deepfakes for banks, marketplaces, and call centers.

## Slide 2 — Problem
- Voice scams + deepfake calls are scaling fast.
- Losses are huge; compliance and reputation risk is bigger.
- Existing tools are transcript-only or manual reviews.

## Slide 3 — Solution
Enterprise pipeline:
1) Upload/stream audio or video
2) Normalize: ffmpeg → WAV mono 16kHz
3) Analyze: acoustic + language signals
4) Output: risk tier + confidence + flags + exportable evidence report

## Slide 4 — Why now
- Cheap voice cloning + global fraud ops
- Pressure tactics work on humans
- Regulation drives proactive monitoring

## Slide 5 — Product
- Web demo (today)
- API + SDK (next)
- Call-center streaming detection (enterprise)

## Slide 6 — Moat
- Multi-signal ensemble (acoustic + semantic + pressure patterns)
- Proprietary dataset plan (scam call corpus + synthetic variants)
- Country/language calibration + client tuning
- Integrations create switching cost (VoIP/Twilio/call centers)

## Slide 7 — Market
Beachhead: call centers + banks (high willingness to pay)
Expand: telecom, insurance, fintech, marketplaces

## Slide 8 — Business model
B2B SaaS:
- Pilot: $10k–$30k
- Annual: $50k–$250k depending on volume/seats
- Usage-based API tier for scale

## Slide 9 — Traction plan (next 90 days)
- 1–3 LOIs with call centers/banks
- Benchmarks: false-positive/negative targets
- First integration partner

## Slide 10 — Ask
$1M to ship:
- Dataset & calibration
- Streaming API + integrations
- 3–5 pilots → annual contracts
