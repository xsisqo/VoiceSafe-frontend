# VoiceSafe — Investor Q&A (hard mode)

## What is your technical moat?
Enterprise normalization (audio/video → WAV mono 16kHz) + multi-signal scoring: acoustic deepfake indicators + language pressure patterns + confidence + exportable evidence.

## Accuracy / false positives?
This is advisory risk scoring (not forensic identity). We calibrate thresholds per client and provide confidence. Focus is catching high-severity fraud while controlling FP cost.

## Competitors?
Most are transcript-only NLP or forensic voice-ID tools. VoiceSafe combines acoustic + semantic signals and packages them into an enterprise workflow + API.

## Why will banks pay?
Fraud losses + reputational and compliance risk. VoiceSafe reduces incidents and speeds investigations via structured reports + case IDs.

## Go-to-market?
Land with call centers/integration partners; expand inside banks. Land-and-expand enterprise motion.

## Privacy / GDPR?
Short retention, configurable retention, encryption, audit IDs. No biometric identity claims.

## Scale?
Separate preprocessing service from inference; stateless inference pods; queue-based workloads; rate limiting.
