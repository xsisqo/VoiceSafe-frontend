What this means
Decision support for fraud prevention
This report summarizes risk signals detected in the provided audio. It is designed to support bank-grade processes: pause, verify, and document.
1) Stop sensitive steps
Do not send money, codes, or approve payments based on voice instructions.
2) Use official channels
Call a verified number from your bank/customer database — not the caller-provided number.
3) Share case & export
Export JSON for audit logs and attach the case to the incident workflow.
